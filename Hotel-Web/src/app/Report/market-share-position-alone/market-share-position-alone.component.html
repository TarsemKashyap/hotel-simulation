Actual market share refers to the market share your hotel receives in a given month. Your hotel’s actual market share is determined by your own business decisions, your competitors’ business decisions and overall business decisions of all hotels located in your area on capital and operating expenditures, room rates, and marketing expenditures.

Market share by positioning alone refers to the market share you would have received if none of the hotels in the market spent any money on marketing. Simply, the market share by positioning alone is determined by your own business decisions, your competitors’ business decisions and overall business decisions of all hotels located in your area on capital and operating expenditures, and room rates. It is the market share you would have received if marketing did not exist.
